We are thrilled to launch our new Classification Agent, an AI assistant that automates classification tasks across all supported question types. This powerful tool helps you handle large-scale projects with ease.

**Key Features:**
- **Batch Processing**: Automatically classify large batches of images (e.g., thousands of vehicle photos) in just a few clicks
- **Save Templates**: Save classification settings as templates to quickly replicate projects
- **Human-in-the-Loop Review**: Built-in review step to verify AI classifications and ensure top quality
- **Seamless Integration**: Works perfectly with existing Labellerr workflows
- **Enterprise-Ready**: Designed to handle enterprise-level classification tasks at any scale

We also improved our tools for team collaboration to make communication faster and more organized.

### Enhanced Notification System

Our new notification system helps your team stay connected and resolve issues quickly.

**Core Features:**
- **Direct Tagging**: Tag team members directly within the annotation interface using the @ symbol
- **Real-Time Alerts**: Tagged members receive instant notifications with a visual indicator
- **Organized Messages**: Use filtering options to manage your messages and stay organized
- **Jump to Label**: Click on a notification to jump directly to the specific label being discussed

### Remarks Tags System

We have enhanced our remarks system to provide a more structured way to give feedback.

**Key Features:**
- **Categorized Feedback**: Use predefined tags like "Mistake" or "Suggestion" to categorize your feedback
- **Streamlined Communication**: Creates a clear and organized feedback loop for annotation projects
- **Better Quality Assurance**: Easily identify and track mistakes and suggestions to improve work quality
